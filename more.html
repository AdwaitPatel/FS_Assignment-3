Joy is the dominant emotion in Riley's mind and takes on the role of the leader of the other emotions. She works tirelessly to ensure that Riley remains happy and tries to steer her away from negative experiences.

Sadness helps Riley process difficult experiences and emotions, such as loss and disappointment. Although initially seen as a hindrance by Joy, Sadness's ability to connect deeply with others becomes vital.

Anger ensures that Riley stands up for herself and expresses her frustrations. He often steps in when things don't go Riley's way or when she faces unfair situations.
